# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and inclusive developer community for neuroscience, BCI, AI/ML, and research software.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and technically constructive.
- Keep discussions focused on public developer resources and integrations.
- Assume good intent, but be precise when discussing correctness or safety.
- Respect privacy, research confidentiality, and security boundaries.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private data, credentials, proprietary source code, or confidential research material.
- Public vulnerability disclosure before coordinated remediation.
- Spam, impersonation, or misleading claims of official certification or partnership.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may moderate issues, discussions, pull requests, or users to protect the quality and safety of the project.

Report conduct concerns to conduct@bcinexus.xyz.
